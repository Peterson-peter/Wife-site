Dyslexia

Dyslexia is a class of learning disablility which is classified by issues with decoding, spelling and cognition of words. It is a disorder which is nuerobilogical in origin and is a results of a deficit in the phonological component of language. Eastside Reading Langaue therpery engages in a multi-sensory word analysis program reading, spelling, and vocabulary. We empower kids to learn by demystifing language strucure, spelling structure and phonoics.

Articulation

As children grow they begin to develop fine moter oral movments required to produce the formation of clear and distint speech sounds. Artiucaltion colunseling is necessary when a child is unable to correctly produce sounds and words at the same level as his peers. Articulation error can be caused by a wide vararity of issues from touge, lip, or jaw movement to untreaded childhood disorders. It is vital to base treamment based on a clinical assement of the invidual child. It is much easier to correct issues during earily intervention rather than after languge patters have solidified.
