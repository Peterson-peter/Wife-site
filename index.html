Dyslexia

Dyslexia is a class of learning disablility which is classified by issues with decoding, spelling and cognition of words. It is a disorder which is nuerobilogical in origin and is a results of a deficit in the phonological component of language. Eastside Reading Langaue therpery engages in a multi-sensory word analysis program reading, spelling, and vocabulary. We empower kids to learn by demystifing language strucure, spelling structure and phonoics.

Articulation

As children grow they begin to develop fine moter oral movments required to produce the formation of clear and distint speech sounds. Artiucaltion colunseling is necessary when a child is unable to correctly produce sounds and words at the same level as his peers. Articulation error can be caused by a wide vararity of issues from touge, lip, or jaw movement to untreaded childhood disorders. It is vital to base treamment based on a clinical assement of the invidual child. It is much easier to correct issues during earily intervention rather than after languge patters have solidified.

Speech and Cognitive Assessment

Reading and speech assements are a complex processed based on the invidual. Assesing, decoding, characterizing and defining a child communication process requires the integration of mutiple streams of information gathered during the assessment process. The result of the assement is a combination of primarily standarized reporting and individual measurements that can be used to to form a corrective action plan. These assments can be used by any accredited Speech Language Pathologist to understand the nature of the indivual disability.

Literacy and Linguistics

Common and quirky words have been a part of English for so long that they no longer fit modern letter-sound correspondence. To learn how to read and spell these old, but useful words, we will use research based strategies to burn them into our mind’s eye, and understand how they impact grammar. Creating and using a Vowel Key to track all 18 English vowel sounds using multi-sensory techniques. Learning how to make non-phonetic words burn into the mind’s eye using the best research based strategies – color coding and mental imagery.
